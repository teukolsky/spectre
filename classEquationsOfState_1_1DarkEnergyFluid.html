Detailed Description

template<bool IsRelativistic>
class EquationsOfState::DarkEnergyFluid< IsRelativistic >

Equation of state for a dark energy fluid.

A dark energy fluid equation of state:

\[ p = w(z) \rho ( 1.0 + \epsilon) \]

where \(\rho\) is the rest mass density, \(\epsilon\) is the specific internal energy, and \(w(z)\) is a parameter depending on the redshift \(z\).

Warning
The numerical implementation requires \(w\neq0\).
