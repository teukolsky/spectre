Detailed Description

A SegmentId labels a segment of the interval [-1,1]. A SegmentId is used to (partially) identify an Element. The segment is the interval [-1 + 2*(N/D), -1 + 2*(N+1)/D] where N = index_ and D = 2 ^ refinement_level_.
