22 /*!
23  * \brief Compute the source terms for the flux-conservative Valencia
24  * formulation of GRMHD with divergence cleaning.
25  *
26  *
27  * A flux-conservative system has the generic form:
28  * \f[
29  * \partial_t U_i + \partial_m F^m(U_i) = S(U_i)
30  * \f]
31  *
32  * where \f$F^a()\f$ denotes the flux of a conserved variable \f$U_i\f$ and
33  * \f$S()\f$ denotes the source term for the conserved variable.
34  *
35  * For the Valencia formulation:
36  * \f{align*}
37  * S({\tilde D}) = & 0\\
38  * S({\tilde S}_i) = & \frac{1}{2} \alpha {\tilde S}^{mn} \partial_i \gamma_{mn}
39  * + {\tilde S}_m \partial_i \beta^m - ({\tilde D} + {\tilde \tau}) \partial_i
40  * \alpha \\
41  * S({\tilde \tau}) = & \alpha {\tilde S}^{mn} K_{mn} - {\tilde S}^m \partial_m
42  * \alpha \\
43  * S({\tilde B}^i) = & -{\tilde B}^m \partial_m \beta^i + {\tilde \Phi}
44  * \gamma^{im} \partial_m \alpha + \alpha {\tilde \Phi} \left( \frac{1}{2}
45  * \gamma^{il} \gamma^{jk} - \gamma^{ij} \gamma^{lk} \right) \partial_l
46  * \gamma_{jk} \\ S({\tilde \Phi}) = & {\tilde B}^k \partial_k \alpha - \alpha K
47  * {\tilde \Phi}
48  * - \alpha \kappa {\tilde \Phi}
49  * \f}
50  *
51  * where
52  * \f{align*}
53  * {\tilde S}^i = & {\tilde S}_m \gamma^{im} \\
54  * {\tilde S}^{ij} = & \sqrt{\gamma} \left[ \left(h \rho W^2 + B^n B_n \right)
55  * v^i v^j + \left(p + p_m \right) \gamma^{ij} - B^n v_n \left( B^i v^j + B^j
56  * v^i \right) - \frac{B^i B^j}{W^2} \right]
57  * \f}
58  *
59  * where \f${\tilde D}\f$, \f${\tilde S}_i\f$, \f${\tilde \tau}\f$, \f${\tilde
60  * B}^i\f$, and \f${\tilde \Phi}\f$ are a generalized mass-energy density,
61  * momentum density, specific internal energy density, magnetic field, and
62  * divergence cleaning field. Furthermore, \f$\gamma\f$ is the determinant of
63  * the spatial metric \f$\gamma_{ij}\f$, \f$\rho\f$ is the rest mass density,
64  * \f$W\f$ is the Lorentz factor, \f$h\f$ is the specific enthalpy, \f$v^i\f$ is
65  * the spatial velocity, \f$B^k\f$ is the magnetic field, \f$p\f$ is the
66  * pressure, \f$p_m = \frac{1}{2} \left[ \left( B^n v_n \right)^2 + B^n B_n /
67  * W^2 \right]\f$ is the magnetic pressure, \f$\alpha\f$ is the lapse,
68  * \f$\beta^i\f$ is the shift, \f$K\f$ is the trace of the extrinsic curvature
69  * \f$K_{ij}\f$, and \f$\kappa\f$ is a damping parameter that damps violations
70  * of the divergence-free (no-monopole) condition \f$\Phi = \partial_i {\tilde
71  * B}^i = 0\f$ .
72  */
